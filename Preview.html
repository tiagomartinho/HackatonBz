Description
Emersy: Informations for emergency team at your hand.

Emersy is a information management systems for emergency services that supports the rescuers to do there job.

The Emersy-Pumps App is a module of Emersy that helps fire fighters to calculate the position of the pumps to bring the water from source to the fire in an optimised way.
